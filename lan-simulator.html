HOW TO USE THIS SIMULATOR
A hands-on sandbox for understanding LAN transmission and switching. There are no scores or wrong answers — every experiment teaches you something.
1

Pick Your Targets

Click any receiver (RX-A, RX-B, RX-C, RX-D) on the diagram to mark it as a target. Click again to deselect. Targeted receivers glow yellow.

2

Choose a Transmission Mode

Pick Unicast, Multicast, or Broadcast. Each one decides who the data is sent to — regardless of your target selection. This is how you discover the difference.

3

Choose a Switching Technique

Pick how the switch processes the frame. This affects how fast and how reliably data passes through. Watch the switch indicator during transmission.

4

Send and Observe

Press Send Data. Packets animate from sender → switch → receivers. The observation panel below explains what you just saw and why it happened.

5

Follow the Lessons (or Skip Them)

The lesson bar at the top suggests experiments. Use ← → to navigate lessons, or ignore them and explore freely.
